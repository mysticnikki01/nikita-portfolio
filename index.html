I turn chaos into
clean, purposeful design.

I'm a UI/UX designer and the founder of MysticBug — which basically means I spend my time turning chaos into clean, functional, and actually good-looking digital experiences.

I have a habit of overthinking everything (yes, everything), but it works in your favour — because every pixel, interaction, and user journey gets questioned, refined, and made better.

I'm obsessed with design that doesn't just look pretty, but actually makes sense. The kind that feels effortless to users but secretly has a lot going on behind the scenes.

When I'm not designing, I'm probably generating new ideas, analysing things I didn't need to analyse, or trying to make something cooler than it needs to be — because "basic" just isn't my thing.
